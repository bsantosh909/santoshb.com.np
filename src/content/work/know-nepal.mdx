## Overview

Know Nepal is an open-data community project: curated, machine-readable
datasets about Nepal — tech companies, government websites, and more — kept
accurate by contributors.

I contribute datasets and maintenance so the information stays current and
useful for anyone building on top of it.

## Where it's heading

Public data in Nepal is scattered and hard to build on — Know Nepal is an
attempt to standardize it. Today it's fully open source and JSON-driven:
plain, versioned datasets anyone can consume directly. The roadmap goes
further — a dedicated API, a web app, a mobile app — once cost and time stop
being the limiting factors. It may well evolve into something bigger along
the way.
