## Overview

The Genesys connects the physical and digital: 3D printing driven over IoT,
with NFTs keeping tamper-proof records of what was made.

I built it full stack — Node.js services and a React + Tailwind frontend,
Raspberry Pi devices orchestrating the printers on the IoT side, and the NFT
layer for on-chain record-keeping.

## Off the beaten path

This one pulled me furthest from typical web work. I learned how 3D printers
actually operate, how to drive them reliably from Raspberry Pis over IoT, and
— on the other end — took a deep dive into NFT minting to put each print's
record on-chain. Physical hardware fails in ways web servers never do, and
building around that was half the education.
