## Overview

My Boat 2 is a ride platform for boats in the US — think ride-hailing, on the
water. It ships as an app ([on the App
Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/my-boat-2/id1582824320)) backed by a full
booking platform.

I built the backend in Node.js and the React-powered frontend experience.

## The interesting bit

Ride-hailing has a classic race condition at its heart: one ride, many
captains, and only one of them can win it. Getting that right — so two people
could never accept the same ride no matter how perfectly their taps collided —
meant thinking hard about locking, request ordering, and all the edge cases
real-time systems love to hide. That problem, more than any single feature,
is what made this project fun.
