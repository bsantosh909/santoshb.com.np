## Overview

Zophal is a social media scheduling and collaboration platform: teams plan
content together, schedule it, and let the platform publish at the right
moment.

I built it full stack — Node.js services on PostgreSQL and Redis,
queue-based background processing that powers the scheduled publishing, a
React + Tailwind frontend, and the AWS infrastructure underneath.

## A modern workflow

Zophal is a work in progress — and a deliberate experiment. It carries every
best practice I've picked up over the years, but the code itself is written
~99% by AI. My job is the part that matters: strict review of every change,
making sure nothing lands that's wrong, insecure, or just badly done. It's
the workflow I believe modern development is heading toward, and this is
where I pressure-test it.
